Description

Flux is 2-4 player party game in which you play as a transcendent crystal being with the power to telepathically move objects around you. The player has to use his telepathic powers to lift and throw objects in the arena to the other players and be the last man standing. The player has the ability to stop any incoming threats by timing his powers, giving the objects extra speed on his next throw.
This project was made in 48 hours for the 2018 Global Game Jam.
